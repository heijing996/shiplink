About ShipLink

Based in Shenzhen, we help factories, trading companies and importers arrange practical freight solutions from China.

Company Profile

ShipLink International Logistics (Shenzhen) Co., Ltd. is based in Longhua, Shenzhen. We mainly serve factories, trading companies and cross-border businesses that need international shipping support from China.

Our services include sea freight, air freight, trucking, customs clearance, warehousing and delivery. If you are not sure whether to use LCL, FCL, air freight or door-to-door delivery, you can send us the cargo details first.

What problems can we help you solve?

Export shipments often become difficult when information is unclear, costs are uncertain or documents are missing close to the shipping date. We help clarify the key points first.

Choose a shipping option

Based on the cargo, destination, timing and budget, we help compare sea freight, air freight, FCL, LCL and door-to-door directions.

Confirm required details

We help identify the usual quote, booking and customs details, such as cargo name, weight, volume, package count, invoice and packing list.

Get a quote direction

Even if the information is not complete, you can start the discussion and receive a practical quotation direction before confirming details step by step.

Have cargo to ship from China?

Send the product name, origin, destination, weight, volume and expected shipping time. Share whatever details you have now, and we will help you judge the shipping option and quote direction.
