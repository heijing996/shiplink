Freight Quote Basics

Knowing the key shipment details makes quote communication faster and more accurate.

Quote details

What details are useful for a sea freight quote?

Product name, package count, weight, volume, origin, destination, trade terms and whether trucking, customs or warehousing is needed.

Shipping method

How should you choose air freight or sea freight?

Urgent, higher-value or smaller shipments often use air freight. Larger cost-sensitive cargo with a wider schedule usually fits sea freight.

Customs documents

What documents are usually checked before customs?

Commercial invoice, packing list, contract and product information are usually reviewed based on the cargo name and destination requirements.
