Freight Services from China

Sea freight, air freight, trucking, customs clearance, warehousing and cross-border logistics support for your China shipments.

Sea Freight

Suitable for larger, cost-sensitive shipments or cargo with a wider delivery window. We can help compare FCL, LCL, port-to-port and door-to-door directions based on volume and destination.

  • From major China ports to major worldwide ports
  • FCL, LCL and general cargo shipment inquiries
  • Port-to-port and door-to-door options can be checked
  • Can be arranged with trucking, customs and warehousing

Air Freight

For urgent, higher-value, smaller-volume or replenishment shipments. We can suggest airport-to-airport and door-to-door air freight quote directions.

  • From major China airports to major global airports
  • Route options can be compared by timing and budget
  • Battery cargo and special cargo need document checks first
  • Airport-to-airport and door-to-door quotes can be requested

Trucking

Factory pickup, port trucking, China-Hong Kong transport, inland transfer and delivery support can be combined with sea freight, air freight and customs clearance.

  • Factory pickup and port trucking
  • China-Hong Kong transport and inland transfer
  • Full truckload and less-than-truckload inquiries
  • Can be arranged together with warehousing and customs

Warehousing & Distribution

If cargo needs temporary storage, split shipments, repacking or waiting for booking confirmation, you can ask about warehousing and delivery arrangements first.

  • Temporary storage and split shipment support
  • Loading, unloading, sorting and labeling
  • Palletizing, reinforcement and packing assistance
  • Distribution and transfer arrangements

Customs Clearance

Before export, we can help check customs declaration details, product information, invoice, packing list and cargo description to reduce last-minute document risks.

  • Export customs document checks
  • Declaration elements and cargo name review
  • Invoice, packing list and contract reminders
  • Special cargo should be confirmed in advance

Small-batch Cross-border Logistics

For samples, small replenishment orders and temporary export shipments. We can suggest air freight, express or dedicated line directions based on weight, volume and timing.

  • Sample and small-batch shipment inquiries
  • Transport mode can be selected by timing need
  • Door delivery options can be discussed
  • Special cargo restrictions should be checked first

One-shipment Planning Support

Not sure whether to ship by sea or air? Not sure which details are required? Send the cargo name, weight, volume, origin, destination and expected shipping time, and we will first help you judge the shipping method, quote information and next steps.

Useful when:

  • This is your first shipment from China
  • Shipment details are not complete yet
  • You want to compare sea, air and door delivery costs
  • Trucking, customs and warehousing may need to be arranged together

Please share first:

  • Cargo name, packages, weight and volume
  • Origin, destination and expected shipping time
  • Trade terms and whether customs or delivery is needed
  • Timing requirement and budget direction
