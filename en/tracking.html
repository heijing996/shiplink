Manual Checking Guide

Send one or more reference numbers and we will verify the shipment with the relevant operation channel.

1. Prepare reference details

BL number, order number, container number, invoice number or shipper and consignee name can help us locate the shipment.

2. Share by direct contact

Send the details by phone, WeChat, WhatsApp or email so our team can manually check with the carrier, airline, warehouse or customs broker.

3. Confirm next actions

After checking, we will explain the current situation and suggest any documents or decisions needed from your side.
